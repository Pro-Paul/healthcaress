What we are

We develop and support the assurance of indicators for the health and social care sector to ensure that the are “fit for purpose” which can be used to determine whether a government policy is carried out, a critical business question or a quality standard is met.  

Our indicators can be used for performance management, benchmarking, quality control, resource planning, regulatory appraisal and to inform commissioners of care and at local level as audit criteria.

We do that by:

- Providing a transparent record of how they are constructed.
- Ensure robust statistical methodologies are used in their development.
- Assure that they answer the question being posed and are suitable for the intended audience.
